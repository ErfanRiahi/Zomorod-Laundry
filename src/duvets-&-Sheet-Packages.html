Steaming Duvets

Even though you might not clean your duvets daily, it is important to do it every once in a while for hygiene reasons. In order to prevent fungus, bacteria and dust mites, it is wise to have your duvet and pillows cleaned at least twice a year. This way, you can sleep well in a fresh, clean bed.

A lot of people prefer to wash their duvets themselves. The problem, however, is that your duvet won’t always fit in your washing machine or dryer. Plus, what often happens is that the machine breaks the lining, or the wool becomes matted. That is why it is smart to have your duvets cleaned professionally. At Dry Cleaning Ocean we are experts at cleaning your duvets and use professional products so your duvets are kept nice and fresh. Down feather duvets stay perfect and even get their bounce back. Woolen duvets should never be washed but have to be steamed. The wool becomes crimped and matted when you wash it, leaving you unable to use your duvet.

Bed sheets and table clothes

It is difficult to wash bed sheets and table cloths yourself because they take up a lot of space and usually do not fit in your washing machine or dryer. Unfortunately, this often damages the bed sheets and table cloths. You can prevent this by having them professionally cleaned. Dry Cleaning Ocean would love to take this task off your hands.
