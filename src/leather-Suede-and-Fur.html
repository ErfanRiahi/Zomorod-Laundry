Cleaning leather, suede and fur

You need a specialist to clean leather, suede and fur. Cleaning leather is fairly challenging. Washing it with water can lead to a change in color, or it can dry out or crack the leather. This is something you want to prevent. Suede is not easy to clean either. It is a special type of leather that is generally made of the skin of sheep, calves or goats. By using chromium salts on the skin, the lovely result is the fabric we call suede. This makes suede sensitive, which means extra care should be taken whilst cleaning it, for example by ensuring it is cleaned at the optimal temperature or by or using a specialized cleaning product.

Lastly, we should talk about cleaning fur. Once again, we do not recommend you do this yourself. Specialized methods and products are required to clean fur, such as a specific type of detergent. That is why Dry Cleaning Ocean would be more than happy to help you clean your leather, suede and fur.
